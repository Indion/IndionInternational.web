Music As A Tool To Cool

“Music is the language of the spirit. It opens the secret of life bringing peace ... , abolishing strife.” I have always felt the need to listen to music while I study or revise because it has always helped me to concentrate better. Many students can’t even revise as it is too quiet. Though some have always found music as a source of distraction and always need silence during their study but researchers say that music helps students to become smarter and increase their capacity for learning and reasoning. There have been multiple studies to support music therapy as part of a holistic approach to healing. One of the most promising impacts of music therapy is seen in children. Music is used to control mood in various swings in our life. Whenever we are sad or in a bad mood listening to music helps us to ignite and make our mood good. Music has always been helpful for me to solve the maze and various specific tasks. I have always found myself to be in a positive mood when I listen to music. It has always helped me to overcome the stress and anxiety of papers and made me prepare more efficiently for exams. It helps me to chill out and have a sound sleep. I think we are more focused and motivated when we are in a good mood which increases one's endurance to study for a longer time and have a good study session. It always positively influences us. Hence, I feel without music life would be a blank to me.

INTER HOUSE COMPETITION

Perfection is not attainable, but if we chase perfection, we can catch excellence. The month of... January unfurled the patriotic feeling in one and all. The school was a witness to the myriads of activities that were conducted for the juniors as well as the seniors. Various Inter-House Competitions were held to mark the occasion. The competitions were a fusion of talent, imagination, creativity, and alertness. Whether it was the melodious solo song and dance instrumental, art, handwriting, creative writing, or debate, the activities helped to bring out the uniqueness of each individual. The students of the Senior School set the stage on fire with their powerful performance for the winning title. The students of all the Eight houses were at their best. Spontaneity and confidence were the hallmarks of the competition. The students left everyone awe-struck as they presented their talent so immaculately and effortlessly. They were applauded for their superb performance. Winners were awarded certificates. The program culminated with a vote of thanks and the rendition of the national anthem.
